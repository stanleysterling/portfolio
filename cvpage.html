Schools I've Been To

The Affiliated High School of Tunghai University Elementary Division

This school is located in Xitun District, Taichung City.
I was in this school during 2009-2010, First Grade.
This school is located on a hill, surrounded by trees, close to nature. It is right inside of the campus of a Christian University, named Tunghai.

Future Heir Bilingual Elementary School

This school is located in Beitun District, Taichung City.
I was in this school during 2010-2015, Second Grade to Sixth Grade.
This school is located in downtown Beitun, surrounded by apartments, and has a very small campus. This is the school that I have been to for the longest period of time, and it's where I've learn the most from.

Mingdao High School Global Citizenship Program

This school is located in Wuri District, Taichung City.
I was in this school during 2015-2016, Seventh Grade.
I was in the international department (named Global Citizenship Program, or GCP) of this local high school. I left when it was changing its system from AP to IB.

St. Viator Catholic High School

This school is located in Beitun District, Taichung City.
I was in this school during 2016-2018, Eighth Grade to Ninth Grade.
This is the best school in the city and I was one of the few ones who passed the transfer exams. I learned most of my academic knowledges here, a steady foundation for my education afterwards.

The Affiliated High School of National Chengchi University

This school is located in Wenshan District, Taipei City.
I was in this school during 2018-2019, Tenth Grade.
After the high school entrance exam, I applied for this school and was successfully admitted. I rented a house there and had a life in an alien city.

Pacific American School

This school is located in Zhubei City, Hsinchu County.
I was in this school during 2019-2021, Eleventh Grade to Twelveth Grade.
This is where I am now! I am now here preparing for college in the United States. Here I learned a lot of things different from all my previous schools.
